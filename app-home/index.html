Google Customer Reviews

Collect post-purchase feedback from your customers through Google's trusted review platform. This app adds a review opt-in link to your checkout thank-you page.

Extension-only Checkout Extensibility

One-Click Setup

Let the app automatically create the required Shopify Page for you. If you prefer, you can also create it manually.

Setup Guide

Follow these steps to configure the app for your store:

1

Configure Checkout Extension

Go to Settings > Checkout in your Shopify admin, then open the Checkout Editor.

  • Switch to the Thank you page
  • Click Add app block
  • Select Google Customer Reviews
  • Configure the settings (see below)
2

Create a Shopify Page

Go to Online Store > Pages and create a new page:

  • Title: Google Customer Reviews (or any title)
  • Handle: google-customer-reviews (must match exactly)
  • Page content can be left empty
3

Enable Theme App Extension

Go to Online Store > Themes > Customize, then open App embeds:

  • Find Google Customer Reviews in the app embeds list
  • Toggle it ON
  • Enter your Google Merchant ID
  • Choose your preferred Opt-in Style
  • Click Save

Checkout Extension Settings

These settings control the banner displayed on the thank-you page:

Default delivery days
Number of days added to the order date to estimate delivery.
Default: 7
Banner heading
The heading text displayed at the top of the banner.
Default: "Google Customer Reviews"
Prompt text
The main message shown in the banner. Supports multiple lines.
Default: "Help us improve by sharing your feedback about your purchase experience!"
Link text
The text displayed on the review link button.
Default: "Leave a Review"

Theme App Extension Settings

These settings are configured in the Theme Editor under App embeds:

Google Merchant ID
Your Google Merchant Center ID. Required for the opt-in module to work.
Required, no default
Opt-in Style
How the Google Customer Reviews opt-in dialog appears on the page.
Default: Center Dialog

Tip: You can find your Google Merchant ID in your Google Merchant Center account. Look for it in the top-right corner of the dashboard.

How It Works

  1. Customer completes checkout and lands on the thank-you page
  2. The app displays a banner with a "Leave a Review" link
  3. Customer clicks the link and is taken to /pages/google-customer-reviews
  4. Google's opt-in module appears, asking if they want to receive a review survey email
  5. After delivery, Google emails the customer to collect their review

Requirements

  • A Google Merchant Center account with Customer Reviews enabled
  • Your store must use Checkout Extensibility (not legacy checkout)
  • The thank-you page must use the new customizable checkout experience
