Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

This privacy policy explains how the Google Customer Reviews app handles data. This app is an extension-only application that does not operate a backend server.

Data We Collect

This app does not collect, store, or process any merchant or customer data on its own servers. The app operates entirely through Shopify's extension framework and does not maintain a database.

Information Passed to Google

When a customer clicks the review link on the thank-you page, the following order information is passed to Google via URL parameters to render the Google Customer Reviews opt-in module:

  • Order ID
  • Customer email address (if available)
  • Country code
  • Estimated delivery date

This data is processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not retain copies of this data.

Third-Party Services

This app integrates with Google Customer Reviews, which is operated by Google LLC. Google's handling of data is governed by their own privacy policy and terms of service. We do not share data with any other third parties.

Data Storage

We do not store any merchant or customer data. All app settings are managed through Shopify's extension framework and stored within the merchant's Shopify admin.

Your Rights

Because we do not store any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete on our end. Merchants can manage app settings and remove the app at any time through their Shopify admin.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the app developer through your Shopify Partner Dashboard.
